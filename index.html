--- layout: default title: Home --- I am a roboticist working at the intersection of imitation learning, planning and decision-making, and machine learning. Broadly, I am interested in enabling robots to address challenging problems in smart and useful ways, and I enjoy getting my hands dirty while working on a wide variety of problems.

I've been lucky to work at a variety of places, including CMU's Robotics Institute, EPFL, Google's self-driving car project, and CSIRO in Australia. You can learn more about my trajectory on my about page, or see cool robots I've worked with on my videos page.
